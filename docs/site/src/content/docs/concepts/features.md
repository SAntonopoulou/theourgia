The canonical feature catalog is **[FEATURES.md](https://github.com/SAntonopoulou/theourgia/blob/main/FEATURES.md)** in the GitHub repository.

It lists ~200 features across 19 categories with status tracking (`[ ]` planned · `[~]` in progress · `[x]` done) and phase cross-references.

## The 19 categories

1. **Time, Calendars & Cosmology** — multi-calendar overlays, multi-tradition astrology, planetary hours, election finder
2. **Journal, Authoring & Blog** — Tiptap editor with magickal blocks, drag-drop templates, body diagrams, audio, library, time-released content, multi-identity
3. **Magical Beings, Relationships & Lineage** — entities with alias-graph merging, offerings, oaths (sealed), initiations (sealed), ancestors, servitors, lineage attestation with cryptographic counter-signing
4. **Divination** — tarot with custom decks, I Ching, geomancy, runes, scrying (trance mode), pendulum, bibliomancy, horary
5. **Practice & Ritual Logs** — templates, dream journal, pathworking, asana/pranayama, banishings
6. **Workshop & Generators** — sigil generator (Spare, Kamea, Rose Cross, hashed, harmonograph, formula-driven), magic squares, talisman designer, magical circle builder, tool registry
7. **Linguistic Tools** — multi-cipher gematria, cross-journal gematria search, transliteration, voces magicae
8. **Body & State** — sensation diagrams, mood/energy snapshots
9. **Synchronicity & Analytics** ("Scientific Illuminism") — quick capture, multi-axis tagging, query builder, visualizations, opt-in network aggregates
10. **Correspondences & Reference** — user-defined tables, CSV import, recipe builder
11. **Bundles, Sharing & Magickal Knowledge Distribution** — Magickal Bundle Format, four sharing modes, piecemeal sharing, bundle signing
12. **Publishing, Monetization & Newsletters** — Stripe Connect, subscription billing, print-quality typography, RSS
13. **Media Library, Calendar Feeds & Maps** — image gallery, audio, video, pilgrimage map, iCal feeds
14. **Federation, Networks & Group Work** — native federation, network hubs, SSO across networks, group ritual coordinator, ActivityPub bridge
15. **Security, Identity & Compliance** — user-choice encryption, GDPR, multi-identity, closed-tradition flags
16. **AI Agent Integration** — opt-in daskalos-pattern (daemon + MCP), BYO keys, never required
17. **Plugin Ecosystem** — SDK, official registry with three trust tiers, sandbox-before-commit
18. **Self-Hosting, Operations & Lifecycle** — one-command deploys, one-click migrations, digital inheritance / memorial mode
19. **Documentation, Testing & Sustainability** — docs from day one, unit/regression/integration/E2E tests at every phase, modal-only alerts

For the detailed enumeration with checkbox status, see [FEATURES.md](https://github.com/SAntonopoulou/theourgia/blob/main/FEATURES.md).
