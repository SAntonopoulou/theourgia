Theourgia is a toolkit for the working magician: a private journal, a
multi-calendar almanac, a ledger of your relationships with spirits and
ancestors, divination instruments, a design workshop, and a small
publishing house — all running on an instance you control. That
instance is your **vault**. Everything you record lives there, under
your name of art, on your terms.

The project is free software (AGPL-3.0), forever. There is no telemetry,
no engagement scoring, and no recommendation feed. Where the software
cannot see something — sealed content above all — it says so plainly
instead of pretending otherwise.

## Principles you can rely on

- **Sealed means sealed.** Content you seal is encrypted on your device
  before it reaches the server. The server cannot read it, cannot search
  it, and cannot recover it if the key is lost. Sealed content is never
  published, never fed to AI features, and never included in feeds or
  exports that promise to exclude it.
- **Honesty by construction.** When a search skips your sealed entries,
  it tells you how many it skipped. When a calendar feed collapses a
  sealed day, it shows a count and nothing else. Nothing is silently
  filtered.
- **No engagement metrics.** Your vault never shows view counts,
  trending lists, or streak-shaming. Numbers appear only where they
  serve the work (a practice streak you asked to track, a sample size
  on a statistic).
- **Your identity is yours.** The vault is signed with your magickal
  name, not your legal identity.

## Where things live

After signing in, the left navigation groups the vault into sections:
Practice (Today, Journal, Daily practice, Practice log), Reference
(Entities, Library, Calendar), Workbench (Divination and the design
tools), Linguistic, Synchronicity & study, Publishing, Media, Network,
and Platform. The [getting started](getting-started.md) guide walks
through each.
